A little about me

Why coding?

As a child I had a natural affinity for technology. Unfortunately, I never had an opportunity to develop it into anything meaningful. I was poor. No one in my family or school saw my potential. I was not the type of kid who thought about the future. At the age of 14 when we moved to England the IT teacher in the school I was assigned to informed me that I am not allowed to pick IT as a side subject because his class is full and he could not support me due to my language barrier. This moment altered the course of my next 8 years and indirectly put me on a path of pursuing business and economics which ultimately I was not interested in as a career.

In late 2021, I had made a decision to quit my full time job to pursue a career in coding. I chose Scrimba’s “Frontend Developer Career Path” as means to achieve that goal. At this point in time, I am nearing the end of this course with the only modules left all being related to React. My long term plan is to become a Full Stack developer. I am genuinely excited to learn as much about coding as possible, however the real fun lies in writing up programs and seeing them function as intended.
