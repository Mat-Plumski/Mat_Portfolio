A little about me

Early Years

As a child I had a natural affinity for technology. I was considered a smart kid by everyone. Unfortunately, my ability to concentrate outside of certain situations have always been inadequate. Consequently I was thought of as lasy and I had no reason to suspect that there is more to it. The enviroment in which I grew up, the type of person that I was and the situation at home together created a metaphorical barrier from a chance of me developing my abilities.

Pivotal Moment

Then at the age of 14 our mother moved with us to England. On the first day at my new school an IT teacher informed me that I am not allowed to pick IT as a side subject because his class is too full and he simply was not able to support me due to my language barrier. I firmly insisted, but to no avail. That one decision moment altered the course of my next 8 years and had indirectly put me on a path of pursuing business and economics at University which ultimately I was not interested in as a career.

Making Things Right

In late 2021, I had made a decision to quit my full time job and pursue a career in coding. I chose Scrimba’s “Frontend Developer Career Path” as means to achieve that goal. At this point in time, I am nearing the end of this course with the only modules left all being related to React. My long term plan is to become a Full Stack developer. I am genuinely excited to learn as much about coding as possible, however the real fun part is writing up code to create a programme and seeing it function as as intended.
